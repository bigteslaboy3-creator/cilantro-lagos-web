About Cilantro

A Destination,
Not Just a Restaurant

Cilantro Lagos is Victoria Island's most spacious dining destination — a world-class terrace restaurant where nature, luxury, and great food meet without compromise. We specialize in farm-fresh steak, authentic hand-stretched pizza and signature curry mains.

"From a quiet lunch to a lively evening of live music and sport, Cilantro adapts to every mood while holding one promise — exceptional quality, every visit."

We source our ingredients daily, cook everything in-house, and obsess over every detail from the first bite to the final glass.
