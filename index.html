About Us

Bold West African Cuisine, Cooked the Right Way.

Our menu is rooted in ancient cooking methods redesigned to meet the modern diner's needs. We are deeply influenced by West African flavors — the kind passed down through generations, perfected over firewood and open flame.

We source our produce fresh daily from local Lagos markets. No frozen stock. No shortcuts. Every ingredient earns its place on the plate. Our kitchen is built on a commitment to quality that you can taste in every single bite.

We offer a warm, relaxed atmosphere where great food meets genuine hospitality. Whether you're dining in or ordering delivery, Chop First promises the same uncompromising standard every time.
